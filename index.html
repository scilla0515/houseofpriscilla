Stewarding Finances with Excellence & Faith

At House of Priscilla, we believe money is not the goal — it is a tool. Inspired by Priscilla of Aquila, a woman in scripture who built a business, opened her home, and stewarded every gift she was given with excellence and faith, this practice is built on that same standard of wisdom, diligence, and grace.

Every person who walks through our doors deserves the same quality of financial guidance that wealthy families have always had access to. Our mission is to help individuals, families, and high-income professionals steward their finances with clarity, strategy, and intention — eliminating debt, building wealth, and planning for a future that reflects their purpose. We bring order where there is confusion and vision where there is uncertainty.

Meet Priscilla

Priscilla Boateng is a Financial Analyst, Masters student in Accounting and Analytics, and founder of The House of Priscilla. At 23, she is building something rare — a faith-rooted financial practice that combines institutional-level analytical expertise with genuine care for the people sitting across the table. She is a Ghanaian-American woman of faith who watched too many hardworking families earn good money and still feel behind — not because they lacked discipline, but because nobody ever sat down and built them a real plan.

Her background in accounting and information systems, combined with her work as a senior financial analyst, gives her a depth of knowledge that most coaches simply do not have. She is currently on the path to her Series 65, CPA, and CFP certifications — building her credentials the right way while building real relationships with real clients who have real goals. Her approach is rooted in stewardship, integrity, and excellence — for every client, at every level.
